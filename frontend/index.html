About this viewer

This interactive map displays regional landslide inventories compiled from multiple geological surveys across Cascadia. The viewer provides consistent filtering, visualization, and download access across heterogeneous datasets.

Washington – DNR Landslide Inventory

Polygon-based mapped landslides derived from field mapping, lidar interpretation, and historical records.

  • Geometry: polygons
  • Attributes: landslide type, confidence, activity, source mapping
  • Coverage varies spatially with lidar availability
Oregon – DOGAMI Landslide Data

Statewide landslide mapping combining detailed studies and reconnaissance mapping.

  • Geometry: points and polygons
  • Attributes standardized for cross-state comparison
  • Variable mapping density by region
California – Geological Survey Inventories

Compiled landslide datasets representing multiple mapping campaigns and vintages.

  • Includes historic and recent failures
  • Heterogeneous attribute completeness
  • Primarily polygon geometries
Canada – Natural Resources Canada

National-scale landslide mapping with variable resolution across British Columbia.

  • Geometry and attribution vary by source region
  • Best used for regional-scale analysis

Note: Mapping completeness and attribute definitions vary by jurisdiction. Absence of mapped landslides does not imply absence of hazard.
